About Us

Welcome To Geez
Hotel

Founded in 2008, Geez Hotel has been redefining fine dining with our innovative approach to classic cuisine. Our restaurant combines timeless elegance with contemporary flair, creating an unforgettable dining experience.

We pride ourselves on using locally-sourced, seasonal ingredients to craft dishes that tell a story. Our wine cellar boasts over 500 selections from around the world, carefully curated to complement our menu.
